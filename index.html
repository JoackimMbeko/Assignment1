COOKIES

Welcome to the house of taste

Cookies are small, sweet baked snacks made mainly from flour, sugar, and fat (usually butter or oil). They are mixed into a dough, shaped into small portions, and baked in an oven until firm.

Basic characteristics Size: Small and flat. Texture: Soft and chewy or crispy and crunchy depending on the recipe. Taste: Sweet, flavored with ingredients like chocolate, vanilla, nuts, or spices.

Cookies are eaten worldwide as: Snacks Desserts Tea or coffee accompaniments In the United States and Canada they are called cookies, while in many other English-speaking places like the UK, they are often called biscuits. Simple definition
